The Visionary

Entrepreneur, Engineer, and Architect of the Future

Elon Reeve Musk

Born June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa, Elon Musk is a technology entrepreneur, investor, and engineer dedicated to revolutionizing transportation both on Earth and in space, advancing sustainable energy, and ensuring the future of humanity through interplanetary colonization.

As the CEO and lead designer of SpaceX, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc., founder of The Boring Company, co-founder of Neuralink, and co-founder of PayPal, Musk has consistently pushed the boundaries of innovation. His vision extends to making humanity a multi-planetary species with SpaceX's Starship program.

With an estimated net worth exceeding $850 billion as of 2026, Musk is committed to using his resources to solve humanity's biggest challenges, from climate change through Tesla's electric vehicles and solar energy solutions to preserving consciousness through Neuralink's brain-computer interfaces.
